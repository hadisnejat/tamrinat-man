Scrambled Eggs

Egss are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recpie for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 butter
  • 2crecam

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and creamin a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds.
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove frm the heat.
